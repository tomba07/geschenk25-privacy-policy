Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 9, 2024

Introduction

Geschenk25 ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

Account Information

  • Username: Required for account creation and login
  • Password: Stored securely using bcrypt hashing (we never store your password in plain text)
  • Display Name: Optional name that appears to other users in groups

Device Information

  • Device Tokens: Unique identifiers for your device to enable push notifications
  • Platform: Your device's operating system (iOS or Android)

App Usage Information

  • Groups: Groups you create, join, or are invited to
  • Group Members: Information about members in your groups
  • Invitations: Group invitations you send and receive
  • Secret Santa Assignments: Random assignments created within groups

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and Maintain Our Service: Authenticate your account, manage your groups, and facilitate Secret Santa assignments
  • Send Push Notifications: Notify you when you receive group invitations
  • Manage Groups: Allow you to create groups, invite members, and manage group activities
  • Facilitate Secret Santa: Create random assignments between group members
  • Improve Our Service: Understand how users interact with the App to enhance functionality

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on our servers hosted by Render. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Password Encryption: All passwords are hashed using bcrypt before storage
  • Secure Connections: All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Database Security: Access to your data is restricted and protected
  • Authentication: We use JWT (JSON Web Tokens) for secure authentication

While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we take reasonable steps to protect your data.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect or process your information:

  • Expo: We use Expo's push notification service to send you notifications about group invitations. Expo processes device tokens to deliver notifications.
  • Render: Our backend services are hosted on Render, which processes and stores your data on our behalf.
  • PostgreSQL: Your data is stored in a PostgreSQL database managed by Render.

These third-party services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review their privacy policies to understand how they handle your information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Within Groups: Your display name (or username if no display name is set) is visible to other members of groups you join
  • Service Providers: We may share information with service providers (like Render and Expo) who help us operate the App
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can access your account information through the App
  • Correction: You can update your display name and other account information at any time
  • Deletion: You can delete your account and associated data by contacting us
  • Push Notifications: You can opt out of push notifications through your device settings
  • Data Portability: You can request a copy of your data by contacting us

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete such information.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have data protection laws that differ from those in your country. By using our App, you consent to the transfer of your information to these countries.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy
  • Notifying you through the App if significant changes are made

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
